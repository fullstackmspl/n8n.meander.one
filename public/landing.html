Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built on a lean, reliable automation stack — no bloated SaaS pricing, no per-message fees.

01
Instant AI Replies

Groq-powered LLM responds to every customer message in under 2 seconds — around the clock, with no human intervention needed.

02
Real-time Dashboard

Every conversation, response status, and performance metric in one clean admin panel — always live, always accurate.

03
Google Sheets Logging

Every message and AI reply is automatically saved to your Google Sheet — searchable, exportable, and yours to keep.

04
n8n Automation

Fully customisable workflow logic for routing, escalation, and alerts — change behaviour without touching a line of code.

05
Failure Alerts

Instant notifications when a message fails to deliver — so no customer query ever slips through unnoticed.

06
Search & Analytics

Search conversations by name, topic, or phone number. Visualise trends, response times, and topic breakdowns.
